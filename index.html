About Me

Being a 15-year-old high schooler, I am seen as cheerful, inquisitive, and risk-taking... and I think that sums me up perfectly. What I don't know, I learn. What I am passionate about, I pursue. And what I want, I strive to achieve. As such, I have self-taught myself, starting at the age of 9, for developing: client-side to server-side - fluent in 8 coding languages. In addition, I have a passion to lead and help others - from leading a 9-day sailing expedition, creating a new club, or co-founding a startup - I have the drive to create change in my community. I am an aspiring entrepreneur, developer, athlete, speech and debater, entrepreneur, and award-winning photographer. I am currently attending Bellarmine College Prep in San Jose and I have my sights set high on the future!

Vision

In the many fields I have pursed; computer science, entrepreneurship, machine learning, geneology, education, and neuroscience; I have continued to build my curiosity for exploration and drive for learning. In my quest to understand, contribute, and create, I believe computer science will eventually weave its way into every department. Whether it be through artificial intelligence, data processing, or alogorithmic modeling, software has proven to be more efficient, accurate, and reliable than human calculations. Currently, I am looking to pursue an intersection of science and entrepreneurship: being able to innovate and distrube for the benefit of society.
